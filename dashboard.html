Executive Summary

Procurement Overview

Total investment of PKR 11.53M across 2 shipped containers (C1: EXP-351, C2: EXP-352) with a 3rd container in planning. 126 unique FMCG products from 25 brands sourced from 12 verified Pakistani suppliers.

Cost Advantage

Average 8.5% savings versus Karachi wholesale rates through bulk procurement and direct supplier relationships. Operating expenses of just PKR 145K represent only 1.3% of procurement cost.

Margin Analysis

Average true margin of 34.8% after landed costs. Top performers include ENO sachets (68.7%), Marhaba herbal products (57.5%), and cosmetics range (54%). Branded food averages 28-35%.

Payment Status

All 12 suppliers fully paid via verified bank transfers (IBFT, Raast, Wire). USD 19,150 wire transfer for international settlement. 22 payment transactions tracked and reconciled.
